# Edge TTS Voice Reference

## 中文普通话

| Voice ID | Gender | Scenario | Style |
|----------|--------|----------|-------|
| `zh-CN-XiaoxiaoNeural` | Female | News, Novel | Warm |
| `zh-CN-XiaoyiNeural` | Female | Cartoon, Novel | Lively |
| `zh-CN-YunjianNeural` | Male | Sports, Novel | Passion |
| `zh-CN-YunxiNeural` | Male | Novel | Lively, Sunshine |
| `zh-CN-YunxiaNeural` | Male | Cartoon, Novel | Cute |
| `zh-CN-YunyangNeural` | Male | News | Professional, Reliable |

## 选择建议

- 默认推荐：`zh-CN-XiaoxiaoNeural`（温暖女声，适合大多数解说/科普内容）
- 专业新闻风格：`zh-CN-YunyangNeural`
- 活泼叙事：`zh-CN-YunxiNeural`（男）或 `zh-CN-XiaoyiNeural`（女）
- 卡通/趣味内容：`zh-CN-YunxiaNeural` 或 `zh-CN-XiaoyiNeural`
- 运动/激情内容：`zh-CN-YunjianNeural`
